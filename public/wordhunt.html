LEADERBOARD





Press the start button to begin the game
Enter any five lettered word,in the box provided and then click the DONE button;The idea is to guess the word which the system has generated within 30 seconds. If you are able to guess the word you win, otherwise a clue will be displayed
Just like this:

  • The letters within the Green boxes are in the original word and at that position itself.
  • The letters within the Yellow boxes are there in the original word but not at that position.
  • The letters within the Red boxes are not there in the original word.
For this Example the word may be smile
A few things to keep a note about:
  • The letters will never be repeated.
  • The words are NOT case sensitive.
  • The words will be simple and meaningful.
The Points Table:
The points table will keep track of the maximum points that can be earned after that attempt.
The Number of attempts will also be kept in track and points will be deducted for each extra attempt.
The counter will keep the track of the time taken to guess the word.
For every 30 seconds 4 points will be deducted.
